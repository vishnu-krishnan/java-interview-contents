Generative AI Mastery Roadmap

Mastering Generative AI requires a deep understanding of AI-driven content creation, covering text, images, videos, and more. Here’s your roadmap to becoming an expert:

1️⃣ Programming Languages – Start with Python and R, essential for AI development.

2️⃣ Mathematics & Statistics – Learn linear algebra, probability, statistics, and calculus for AI modeling.

3️⃣ Data Handling – Master data preprocessing, cleaning, and augmentation to prepare high-quality datasets.

4️⃣ Machine Learning Fundamentals – Understand supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning techniques.

5️⃣ Deep Learning Essentials – Explore neural networks (ANNs, CNNs, RNNs), activation functions, and backpropagation.

6️⃣ Understanding Generative AI – Learn about Generative vs. Discriminative Models and real-world applications.

7️⃣ Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) – Study autoencoder architectures used in image reconstruction and anomaly detection.
